Custom Clearance

Customs Brokerage is the most important part of overseas transportation for both importers and exporters. Businesses are approaching customs brokerage companies for easy approval of documents from various govt. agencies for importing and exporting goods across the world. We are one such company with expertise in the field of Customs brokerage for a longer period of time.

Our experts ensure that all our staff is constantly updated regarding new trade policies and customs laws and regulations covering all aspects of customs processes, including import/export clearance, declarations, bond requirements, and duty rates.

We efficiently manage entire procedural works in the least possible time that excludes cost overruns to clients due to delays. All these services are highly required particularly for high-value shipments.

We offer the below services as part of our customs brokerage solutions:

  • Import clearances - Air/Sea
  • Export clearances - Air/Sea
  • Project Registration
  • ATA Carnet
  • AOG Clearances
  • Reverse Logistics
  • Advance Authorization

Consultancy

Every business involved in the movement of goods from one country to another results in customs duties and taxes imposed on these shipments. Customs duties and taxes can be expensive unless it is managed and controlled through careful and timely planning. Companies which recognize not only the benefits of customs planning but the necessity of undertaking it as an integral part of the total business planning process, gains both financial savings and operational benefits. In this scenario, consulting an expert in the industry is the norm to benefit from lower costs.

Over the years, we have gained adequate knowledge in the customs domain. We have developed in-depth knowledge of the administrative and operational demands moving goods globally. At Vakkiyl, we have set up our consultancy team to assist and advise in preparing documented procedures, saving time and effort during customs audits and the initial approval process.

We extend our Consultancy services to:

  • Customs consultancy
  • SVB - Special Valuation Branch
  • AEO program
  • Refunds
  • GST consultancy
  • STPI/SEZ services
  • STPI Consultancy
  • SEZ Consultancy
